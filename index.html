Our Restaurant Origins

Italia Pastria, nestled in the heart of Siena, Italy, has been delighting guests with authentic Italian flavors since the early 1900s. Founded by Giovanni Rossi, our restaurant began as a humble trattoria dedicated to homemade pasta and fresh, local ingredients. Passed down through generations, Italia Pastria remains a beloved culinary destination, where tradition and passion create unforgettable dining experiences. Join us and become part of our rich, delicious legacy.
